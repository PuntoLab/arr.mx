  • Services
    • HOW CAN WE HELP YOU?

    In ARR LAWFIRM, we have the knowledge and experience to guide you in the following areas:

    LABOR MATTER .- Any matter relating to the possible contingencies that may arise between workers and the employer, either trusted or union. Demands of workers, collective bargaining labor contract negotiations with union, etc.

    CONSTITUTIONAL MATTER.- Sometimes is necessary to apply for a constitutional injunction by some companies or individuals; either from acts of authority (indirect), or against sentences that violate the law of Mexico; or unconstitutional acts affecting the human rights of the client.

    PUBLIC NOTARY.- The legal system in Mexico is endowed with formalities that made necessary the intervention of a public notary, which gives public faith in legal acts, are examples of this:

    • Powers of attorney
    • Incorporation of a company (business creation).
    • Sale of property (houses, buildings, land.).
    • Assemblies of corporations, among others.


    ADMINISTRATIVE MATTER.- Advice and appropriate preparation of documents in administrative matters, such as those related to Mexican Social Security Institute (notice of change of address, work stoppage, etc.), the National Migration Institute (processing and renovation FMM, working visa, adjustments) and any other agency of an administrative nature.

    CRIMINAL MATTER.- Complaints and appeals to the Public Ministry and follow the corresponding trial before the appropriate criminal court.

    COMMERCIAL MATTER.- legal collection, to the clients who not pay to the customer.

    CIVIL MATTER.- Any matter relating to the Civil Code of the State of Guanajuato and the Federal Civil Code, including family matters (divorces, adoptions, etc.); including all the types of contracts and obligations that may agree legal entities or persons.

    CONSULTING AND MANAGEMENT OF PROCEDURES (LOCAL, STATE AND FEDERAL).- Advice and management to agencies in all three levels of government, examples of these are procedures with Ministry of Economy (registration notices to the Register of Foreign Investment, application to get IMMEX and PROSEC programs); state procedures like applications for Public Record of the Property and Commerce, and municipal procedures such as transfer of ownership, local permits, etc.

    REVIEW AND ELABORATION OF DOCUMENTS.- All types of contracts (lease, services, commercial, constructions), verifying that comply with the provisions of relevant laws, domestic civil code, commercial code, tax code, etc.
