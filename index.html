  • Servicios
    LO QUE HACEMOS

    En ARR ABOGADOS, contamos con el conocimiento y experiencia para orientarlo en las siguientes materias:

    MATERIA LABORAL.- Cualquier asunto relacionado con las posibles contingencias que puedan suscitarse entre los trabajadores y el patrón, ya sea de confianza o sindicalizados. Demandas de trabajadores, negociaciones del contrato colectivo de trabajo, negociación con sindicato, etc., Revisiones de la secretaria del trabajo local y federal.


    Nos caracteriza la profesionalidad en la prestación de todos nuestros servicios.


    JUICIO DE AMPARO.- En ocasiones es necesario solicitar un amparo por parte de algunas empresas o personas; ya sea contra actos de autoridad (amparo indirecto), amparo contra leyes (amparo indirecto) como lo es el caso del Derecho al Alumbrado Público o en general “impuestos” inconstitucionales que afecten el erario del cliente, y amparo directo (contra resoluciones o sentencias dentro de los procesos que tenga la empresa).


    MATERIA NOTARIAL.- El sistema jurídico en México está dotado de solemnidades para las que son necesarias el actuar de un Notario Público, el cual otorga fé pública a los actos que el suscribe, ejemplos de esto son: otorgamiento de poderes, constitución de sociedades mercantiles (creación de empresas), compraventa de inmuebles, asambleas de sociedades mercantiles, entre otras.


    MATERIA ADMINISTRATIVA.- Asesoría y en su caso elaboración de documentos en materia administrativa, como los relacionados con el Instituto Mexicano del Seguro Social IMSS (aviso de cambio de domicilio, suspensión de labores, etc.),


    MATERIA PENAL.- Presentación de denuncias y recursos ante el Ministerio Público y en su caso seguimiento de juicio ante el juzgado penal correspondiente.


    MATERIA MERCANTIL.- Cobranza judicial y extra judicial, respecto a los posibles clientes que incurran en mora, o no paguen debidamente al cliente.


    MATERIA CIVIL.- Cualquier asunto relacionado con el código civil, como lo puede ser la materia familiar; sin omitir los tipos de contratos y obligaciones en que pueden incurrir las personas morales o físicas.


    ASESORIA Y GESTION ANTE DEPENDENCIAS LOCAL, ESTATAL Y FEDERAL.- Asesoría y gestión ante dependencias de los tres niveles de gobierno, ejemplos claros de estos son trámites ante Secretaria de Economía (avisos, programas de fomento IMMEX y PROSEC), estatales como lo son ante cualquier secretaria de gobierno del estado o registro públicos del estado de Guanajuato, y municipales ante cualquier dependencia, algunos ejemplos son el predial, catastro, traslado de dominio, permisos locales.


    REVISION Y ELABORACIÓN DE DOCUMENTOS.- revisión y elaboración de todo tipo de contratos (arrendamiento, de servicios, mercantiles), verificando que se cumplan con lo establecido en leyes correspondientes, código civil, código de comercio, código fiscal, etc.


    DERECHO MIGRATORIO.- En ARR abogados contamos con asesoría y gestión ante el Instituto Nacional de Migración (INM) como lo son:
    • Visa de trabajo.
    • Permiso para trabajar. (Estancia menor a 6 meses.)
    • Renovación de Forma Migratoria Múltiple (FMM).
    • Registro de la empresa ante INM (Constancia de empleador).
    • Regularización de situación migratoria.
    • Visas por unidad familiar (Esposa, Hijos, etc.)
    • Obtención de permiso para viaje.
    • Entre otros.

  • Services
    HOW CAN WE HELP YOU?

    In ARR LAWFIRM, we have the knowledge and experience to guide you in the following areas:

    LABOR MATTER .- Any matter relating to the possible contingencies that may arise between workers and the employer, either trusted or union. Demands of workers, collective bargaining labor contract negotiations with union, etc.

    CONSTITUTIONAL MATTER.- Sometimes is necessary to apply for a constitutional injunction by some companies or individuals; either from acts of authority (indirect), or against sentences that violate the law of Mexico; or unconstitutional acts affecting the human rights of the client.

    PUBLIC NOTARY.- The legal system in Mexico is endowed with formalities that made necessary the intervention of a public notary, which gives public faith in legal acts, are examples of this:

    • Powers of attorney
    • Incorporation of a company (business creation).
    • Sale of property (houses, buildings, land.).
    • Assemblies of corporations, among others.


    ADMINISTRATIVE MATTER.- Advice and appropriate preparation of documents in administrative matters, such as those related to Mexican Social Security Institute (notice of change of address, work stoppage, etc.), the National Migration Institute (processing and renovation FMM, working visa, adjustments) and any other agency of an administrative nature.

    CRIMINAL MATTER.- Complaints and appeals to the Public Ministry and follow the corresponding trial before the appropriate criminal court.

    COMMERCIAL MATTER.- legal collection, to the clients who not pay to the customer.

    CIVIL MATTER.- Any matter relating to the Civil Code of the State of Guanajuato and the Federal Civil Code, including family matters (divorces, adoptions, etc.); including all the types of contracts and obligations that may agree legal entities or persons.

    CONSULTING AND MANAGEMENT OF PROCEDURES (LOCAL, STATE AND FEDERAL).- Advice and management to agencies in all three levels of government, examples of these are procedures with Ministry of Economy (registration notices to the Register of Foreign Investment, application to get IMMEX and PROSEC programs); state procedures like applications for Public Record of the Property and Commerce, and municipal procedures such as transfer of ownership, local permits, etc.

    REVIEW AND ELABORATION OF DOCUMENTS.- All types of contracts (lease, services, commercial, constructions), verifying that comply with the provisions of relevant laws, domestic civil code, commercial code, tax code, etc.

LABOR MATTER .- Any matter relating to the possible contingencies that may arise between workers and the employer, either trusted or union. Demands of workers, collective bargaining labor contract negotiations with union, etc.

CONSTITUTIONAL MATTER.- Sometimes is necessary to apply for a constitutional injunction by some companies or individuals; either from acts of authority (indirect), or against sentences that violate the law of Mexico; or unconstitutional acts affecting the human rights of the client.

PUBLIC NOTARY.- The legal system in Mexico is endowed with formalities that made necessary the intervention of a public notary, which gives public faith in legal acts, are examples of this:

  • Powers of attorney
  • Incorporation of a company (business creation).
  • Sale of property (houses, buildings, land.).
  • Assemblies of corporations, among others.


ADMINISTRATIVE MATTER.- Advice and appropriate preparation of documents in administrative matters, such as those related to Mexican Social Security Institute (notice of change of address, work stoppage, etc.), the National Migration Institute (processing and renovation FMM, working visa, adjustments) and any other agency of an administrative nature.

CRIMINAL MATTER.- Complaints and appeals to the Public Ministry and follow the corresponding trial before the appropriate criminal court.

COMMERCIAL MATTER.- legal collection, to the clients who not pay to the customer.

CIVIL MATTER.- Any matter relating to the Civil Code of the State of Guanajuato and the Federal Civil Code, including family matters (divorces, adoptions, etc.); including all the types of contracts and obligations that may agree legal entities or persons.

CONSULTING AND MANAGEMENT OF PROCEDURES (LOCAL, STATE AND FEDERAL).- Advice and management to agencies in all three levels of government, examples of these are procedures with Ministry of Economy (registration notices to the Register of Foreign Investment, application to get IMMEX and PROSEC programs); state procedures like applications for Public Record of the Property and Commerce, and municipal procedures such as transfer of ownership, local permits, etc.

REVIEW AND ELABORATION OF DOCUMENTS.- All types of contracts (lease, services, commercial, constructions), verifying that comply with the provisions of relevant laws, domestic civil code, commercial code, tax code, etc.
